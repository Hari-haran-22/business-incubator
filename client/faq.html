FAQ

Answers to the questions new founders usually ask first.

This page helps visitors understand how the platform works before they commit time to applying or browsing the network.

You can expand this page over time as users ask the same questions about eligibility, response times, mentor quality, and investor access.

Who is this platform for?

Business Incubator is designed for early-stage founders, operators who mentor startups, and investors looking for structured visibility into emerging teams.

Do founders need traction before joining?

No. Founders can join at the idea, prototype, MVP, growth, or scale stage. The goal is to show where the company is today and what support it needs next.

How do mentor requests work?

Startup founders browse the mentor network, open a profile, and send a structured request describing what they are building and what help they need.

What do investors see?

Investors can review startup profiles, understand stage and funding needs, and save interesting companies to their portfolio view for follow-up.

Are resources free?

The resource library can contain free, paid, or freemium tools and guides. The labels on each card help founders quickly judge fit.

Can I use this as a public incubator website?

Yes. The trust pages, contact information, stories, and public marketplace pages now make the project look much more like a complete incubator website.
