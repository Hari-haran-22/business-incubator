Privacy policy

How platform data is collected and used.

This page gives the website an essential trust layer and sets a better baseline for handling founder, mentor, and investor information.

Information we collect

We collect account information such as name, email address, role, profile details, and content you submit to the platform, including startup, mentor, and resource information.

How we use information

We use platform data to provide core functionality, match mentors and founders, enable investor discovery, improve the product experience, and support user communication.

Sharing and visibility

Certain information is visible to other users depending on platform roles and product features. Public and internal visibility should be reviewed before uploading sensitive business details.

Contact

Questions about data handling can be directed to the incubator team through the contact page or your configured support email address.
